Web Development

Fussy Kitchen is a Soul cuisine restaurant in Liverpool's city centre. Customers have the choice of eating in, picking up, or taking their food away from the restaurant.

Client: Fussy Kitchen
Technologies: WooCommerce, Wordpress

The client requested that a website be created that would allow customers to place orders online. It was my responsibility to design a user-friendly website that allowed users to simply place orders on the platform.

The website is built with Wordpress and Woo commerce, allowing people with little to no online experience to simply modify the menu and website.
