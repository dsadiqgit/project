Web Development

Club Au is a gold-for-cash website. After doing A/B tests, I collaborated closely with a UX designer to construct web components and update the website to fulfil the demand.

Client: Club Au
Technologies: HTML, Scss, JavaScript, PHP

Club Au offers fast cash for gold in 3 simple steps. The customers orders a free gold seller's pack online. They must then send their jewellery via the pre-paid envelope. Once the pack is recieved it processed within 24 hours and the customer is sent an email and SMS with an offer.

The idea of trust was a significant component the customer wished to have on the website. The website was integrated with live trust pilot reviews.
